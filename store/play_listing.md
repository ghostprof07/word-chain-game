**Full description** (max 4000):
```
Lexicoil is a fast, competitive word chain game. Each word must start with the
last letter of the previous one — build the longest, highest-scoring chain before
the clock runs out.

PLAY YOUR WAY
• Online multiplayer — create a room, share the code, and duel a friend in real time.
• Vs Bot (offline) — challenge a bot on Easy, Medium or Hard. No internet needed.
• Training (solo) — practice at your own pace and beat your best score.

FEATURES
• 6 languages for both the interface and the dictionary: English, Turkish, German,
  Spanish, French, Russian.
• Letter-based scoring — rarer letters are worth more, so smart words win.
• Turn timer and total match timer keep every game tense and quick.
• Live in-game chat with your opponent.
• Clean, colorful design with sound effects and smooth animations.
• Personal stats: games, wins, losses, words played and your best word.

Simple to learn, hard to master. How long can your chain get?
```
