Lexicoil — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Lexicoil is a word-chain game. You can play online against another person, offline against a bot, or solo in Training mode. This policy explains what data the app handles.

What we collect

  • Display name — the nickname you type before a game. You may use any nickname; it does not need to be your real name.
  • Chat messages — only in online games, the messages you send to your opponent.

These are used only to run a multiplayer match: your nickname and messages are relayed through our game server to the other player in your room. Offline modes (vs Bot and Training) send nothing — they run entirely on your device.

What we do NOT collect

No real name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or tracking identifiers. The app has no ads, no analytics, and no user accounts.

Storage and retention

An online game room exists temporarily in the server's memory only while a match is in progress, and is discarded when the match ends or players leave. Chat messages are relayed in real time and are not stored permanently. Your best Training score is saved locally on your own device only.

Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. The only data transfer is relaying your nickname and chat messages to the opponent in your own game room.

Word definition lookups

If you tap a word to see its meaning, that single word is sent to a public dictionary service (TDK for Turkish, dictionaryapi.dev for English, or Wiktionary for other languages) to fetch the definition. No other data is sent, and the word is not linked to you.

Children

Lexicoil is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. Please do not share personal information in chat.

Changes

We may update this policy; the date above will change accordingly.
